BUILDING THE FUTURE


Launched in 2017 by software developer and entrepreneur Andrés Montenegro with the goal of developing the best software systems and digital solutions in the market at the most affordable rates possible, AM Software focuses on value, quality, scalability and security at all times, while providing the most reliable service and products to our clients, who are always at the center of our business and operations. Since its launch, AM Software has been providing high-value digital solutions to businesses and organizations of all sizes across many industries around the world, very often uncovering a whole new world of possibilities that were not previously available to them, because of the many barriers to entry prevalent in the industry today, the main one being affordability of high-quality service. Before launching AM Software, Andrés Montenegro worked in several areas of Software Engineering, Information Technology and Management for companies from different countries since 2010, which allowed him to develop an immensely rich and eclectic range of skills beyond average software development and ultimately decide to dive fully into entrepreneurship and start AM Software from the ground up by self-funding, investing life savings along the way, his vision of “Building The Future” by bridging the gaps that exist in today’s digital world by providing the latest technological tools and services to businesses and organizations of any size and industry around the world, with the highest quality at the most affordable rates possible and on a personalized, case-by-case basis.

From 2019 to mid-2020, AM Software largely turned its focus into developing ever more advanced software projects, both independently and for private companies, with long term development and release plans. Unfortunately, because of the sudden rise of COVID-19 pandemic and its effects around the world, a fair share of these projects were put on hold momentarily. However, we managed to turn this unpredictable situation into an opportunity to innovate and help the maximum number of businesses, brands, organizations and individuals around the world during these difficult times. By thoroughly and creatively applying the experience, skills and tools developed during this enriching one and a half year period of sacrifice, tireless development and innovation, we were able to vastly upgrade and optimize our service infrastructure, allowing us to serve our clients in an even more personalized way, with our Digitize program and our AM Software Club Platform.
